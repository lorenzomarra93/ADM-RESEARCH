# Research Questions

## Analysis-Focused
1. How do ADM metadata parameters (position, divergence, spread) correlate with perceived timbral depth?
2. What descriptors best discriminate between object-based and bed-based spatial strategies?
3. Which visualization techniques communicate spatial density without obscuring musical form?

## Creation-Focused
4. How can parameter-space mappings reflect micro, meso, and macro structural decisions simultaneously?
5. Which control strategies best translate gestural controllers into ADM-OSC trajectories for ensemble writing?
6. How can score-based notation capture immersive spatial intent alongside harmonic and rhythmic data?

## Cross-Cutting
7. What feedback metrics ensure that analytical insights genuinely inform compositional choices?
8. How can film and concert case studies share a core mapping framework while meeting domain-specific constraints?
9. Which reproducibility practices guarantee that artistic experiments remain auditable over the course of the PhD?

Add or refine questions as new experiments require sharper focus. Each major task should reference at least one question ID.
